Privacy Policy

Protecting your personal data is important to us. Therefore, we process any data exclusively in accordance with applicable legal regulations (GDPR, TMG). This privacy policy outlines the key aspects of data handling on the OpenPSG website.

1. General Information

OpenPSG does not use cookies and does not collect any personal data beyond what is technically necessary to operate the website.

2. Collection and Processing of Personal Data

No personal data is stored or processed when you visit OpenPSG, except for minimal technical data required to ensure the basic functionality and security of the website (e.g., your IP address). These are automatically handled by the server and deleted after the session ends.

3. Legal Basis for Processing

Any processing of technical data is done solely on the basis of legal regulations, particularly Article 6 (1) (f) of the GDPR (legitimate interest). Our legitimate interest lies in the secure, stable, and efficient delivery of our website.

4. No Disclosure of Data to Third Parties

We do not share personal data with third parties. Your data is not transferred to third countries.

5. Data Security

We take appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your data against unauthorized access, alteration, or loss. However, complete security cannot be guaranteed due to the nature of the internet.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to request information about any personal data we may have about you, as well as the right to rectification, deletion, or restriction of processing. You may also object to data processing based on legitimate interest.

7. Contact

If you have questions regarding the processing of personal data or this privacy policy, please refer to our contact information listed in the Impressum.

8. Updates to This Privacy Policy

We reserve the right to update this privacy policy as needed to comply with legal changes or modifications to the site. The current version is always available on this page.
